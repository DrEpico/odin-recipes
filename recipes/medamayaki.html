Medamayaki

Description

Medamayaki is a Japanese name of what is called fried egg in English speaking countries.

Ingradients

  • 2 Eggs
  • pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Crack the egg and put its inside on a pan
  2. After a minute of cooking it, put some salt and pepper
  3. After another minute, put the frying pan lid on, and leave it as that for another minute
  4. Enjoy!
