Sushi

Description

Sushi is one of the most loved Japanese foods in and outside of Japan. It is made of a piece of raw fish on a prepared rice.

Ingradients

  • Salmon fillet or tuna fillet
  • Rice (preferably the Japanese rice or the stickier ones
  • Vinegar
  • soya sauce
  • wasabi

Steps

  1. Mix 5ml of Vinegar in 200g of cooked rice
  2. Gently shape the rice in about 3cm x 8cm using hands
  3. Put the fish meat on the rice
  4. Enjoy with soya sauce and a little but of wasabi!
